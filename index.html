Who I Am

My name is Tyler Brown, and I am a web designer from Central Ohio. I have education and work experience in both web/visual design, along with several years of frontend development, content entry, and quality checking experience to launch HTML/CSS and WordPress sites alike. I graduated from The Ohio State University with a BA in Communication Technology and a minor in Design Thinking.

What I do

I create web designs with a focus on user experience and use my experience with front-end devleopment to do so. This is my wheelhouse:

Web Development

I have experience in creating sites using plain HTML/CSS, utilizing flexbox and other responsive technologies. I also have experience in creating custom marketing emails from HTML/CSS.

Web Content Production

I have extensive experience in content entry using CMS programs such as Tridion and WordPress, along with quality checking and meta data editing to improve SEO results. I also have experience with preparing site content with tools like Adobe Photoshop.

Web Design

From simple mock-ups to launching WordPress sites designed with the user first, I can assist in any step of the process.
